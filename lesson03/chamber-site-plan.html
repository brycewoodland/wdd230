Salt Lake City Chamber

Site Purpose:

The purpose of this chamber of commerce is to provide opportunities for networking. This is done by advocating and promoting events related to business in nearby areas. It's also to promote those businesses and create an environment where they can flourish and grow. On another note, it is to allow those in the area to build a stronger community. Lastly, it attracts visitors and tourists alike by sharing local attractions tourists would be interested in.

Scenarios:

Scenario 1: A new business owner is searching for ways to become more involved and integrated within the community. They would like to reach a larger audience and feel a sense of belonging. This challenge of networking would allow them to obtain advice and help.

Scenario 2: This person is moving to Salt Lake City and wants to learn more about the city and community. They want to reach out to fellow members of the chamber of commerce and feel welcomed within their new community. This will also give them an overview of local events taking place.

Scenario 3: Someone who is visiting Salt Lake City as a tourist and would like to get to know the area. There would be a list of activities going on that would allow them to have some options on what they could do.
